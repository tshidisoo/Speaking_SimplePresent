📚 Daily Routines Dialogue

Listen, Learn, and Practice Simple Present Tense

🎧 Listen to the Dialogue

👩‍🏫 Teacher
Good morning, class! Today we'll talk about daily routines. Let's listen to Anna and James.
👩 Anna
Hi James! What time do you usually wake up?
👨 James
I usually wake up at 6:30 in the morning. What about you?
👩 Anna
I wake up at 7 o'clock. Do you eat breakfast every day?
👨 James
Yes, I do. I usually eat eggs and bread. Do you eat breakfast?
👩 Anna
Sometimes. I usually drink tea, but I don't always eat food in the morning.
👨 James
After breakfast, I go to school. Do you walk or take the bus?
👩 Anna
I usually take the bus. What about you?
👨 James
I walk, because I live near the school.
👩 Anna
What do you do after school?
👨 James
I do my homework and then I play football. Do you play sports?
👩 Anna
No, I don't. I like reading books.
👨 James
What time do you go to bed?
👩 Anna
I go to bed at 10 o'clock.
👨 James
I go to bed a little later, around 11 o'clock.

📖 Grammar Tips - Simple Present Tense

Positive Form:

I/You/We/They + base verb
He/She/It + verb + s/es

Examples:
• I wake up at 7 o'clock.
• She eats breakfast every day.

Negative Form:

I/You/We/They + don't + base verb
He/She/It + doesn't + base verb

Examples:
• I don't eat breakfast.
• He doesn't play sports.

Question Form:

Do + I/you/we/they + base verb?
Does + he/she/it + base verb?

Examples:
• Do you walk to school?
• Does she like reading?
